# SacredScripture Quick Start

Use this one-page guide to launch your first worship video in minutes.

## Step 1: Create Your Account
- Visit the SacredScripture sign-in page.
- Create an account and start the free trial.

## Step 2: Choose a Verse
- Enter the verse reference.
- Paste the verse text.
- Pick a mood (Peace, Hope, Reflection, Worship, Reverence).

## Step 3: Generate
- Select a duration (3, 5, or 10 minutes).
- Click Generate.

## Step 4: Download
- Download the MP4 for visuals.
- Download the WAV audio if you want the soundscape.

## Step 5: Use in Worship Software
- ProPresenter: add the MP4 as a background loop.
- OBS: add the MP4 as a media source.
- MediaShout: add the MP4 to your service playlist.

## Optional: Invite Your Team
- Share credentials or create a shared account for the worship team.

## Support Checklist
- If generation fails, verify your plan limits.
- If preview does not load, refresh the dashboard and retry.
- If audio is missing, confirm the download location.
